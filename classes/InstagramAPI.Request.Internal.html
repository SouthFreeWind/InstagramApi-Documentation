_uploadVideoChunks()

_uploadVideoChunks(integer  $targetFeed, \InstagramAPI\Request\Metadata\Internal  $internalMetadata) : \InstagramAPI\Response\UploadVideoResponse

Performs a chunked upload of a video file, with support for retries.

Note that chunk uploads often get dropped when their server is overloaded at peak hours, which is why our chunk-retry mechanism exists. We will try several times to upload all chunks. The retries will only re-upload the exact chunks that have been dropped from their server, and it won't waste time with chunks that are already successfully uploaded.
